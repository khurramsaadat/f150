Car Buying Tips

Required Documents

  • UAE Residence Visa
  • Emirates ID
  • Passport Copy
  • Dubai-Issued Driving License
  • Car Insurance Agreement

Before Viewing

  • Research market prices for the specific model and year
  • Compare prices across multiple platforms
  • Check vehicle history and service records
  • Verify seller information and documentation
  • Request detailed photos of the vehicle

During Inspection

  • Always inspect the vehicle in person
  • Consider having a mechanic check the vehicle
  • Check for any signs of accidents or repairs
  • Verify all electronic features are working
  • Inspect tires and brake condition

Documentation

  • Check RTA registration and fines
  • Verify warranty coverage if applicable
  • Ensure all service records are available
  • Verify insurance status and requirements
  • Check for any outstanding loans on the vehicle

Test Drive

  • Test drive before making a decision
  • Check for unusual sounds or vibrations
  • Test all gears and driving modes
  • Check acceleration and braking performance
  • Test AC and other comfort features

Best Time to Buy

  • During Ramadan - huge discounts and special campaigns
  • Summer season - less demand, better deals
  • Year-end - clearance of old models
  • National holidays - special packages and offers

Registration & Costs

  • New car registration: AED 420
  • Traffic file opening: AED 220
  • Vehicle Technical Inspection: AED 170
  • Transfer of Ownership: AED 350
  • Late registration fine: AED 25/month

Insurance Information

  • Mandatory requirement for all vehicles
  • Costs 1.25% to 3% of car's value
  • Factors: age, credit score, driving history
  • Compare multiple insurance quotes online

Used Car Considerations

  • Cars depreciate 20-30% in first year
  • Inspection required for cars over 3 years
  • Check vehicle's previous registration card
  • Verify no outstanding loans on vehicle
  • Both parties must visit Traffic Police for transfer

Source: Information from Property Finder UAE
